About SolvvSpace

Small enough to care.
Large enough to deliver.

A Bedford-based IT and HR consultancy with one purpose — to help businesses cut through complexity, adopt the right technology, and build the foundations to grow with confidence.

Who we are
A team of specialists who put your business first

At SolvvSpace, our team of highly skilled IT and HR consultants brings deep expertise across cloud computing, bespoke development, business solutions, cyber security, and managed services. We don't believe in one-size solutions — we take the time to understand how your organisation works before we recommend anything.

Our mission is to enhance your operational efficiency and drive growth in a rapidly evolving digital landscape. As a Microsoft Gold Partner, we combine world-class technology with the kind of personal service that larger firms simply can't offer.

How we work
Your journey with SolvvSpace
Every engagement follows a clear, collaborative process — because technology only succeeds when the people using it are fully on board.
1
Discovery
Step one

Getting to know you

It's not just about the technology. Before we recommend a single tool, we invest time in understanding how you and your team actually work — your culture, your goals, your frustrations, and your ambitions. Understanding what you want to achieve and the strategic context of your business is just as important as any platform decision. This is where great outcomes begin.

2
Planning
Step two

First steps together

Taking the first steps towards digital transformation can feel daunting — there are so many options and so many different routes. We make sure you fully understand the process and the outcome of every choice before committing. We help you manage change within your team, define clear expectations, and plan for a smooth project where you start seeing return as quickly as possible.

3
Delivery
Step three

Working with you

We don't hand over and walk away. We support the change, deliver training to empower your staff, and share a wealth of experience and good practice to ensure your project succeeds — and that you maintain momentum long after go-live. Your team's confidence in the new way of working is as important to us as the technology itself.

4
Partnership
Step four

Transform and build success

For SolvvSpace, it's all about the relationships we build with our clients. Together we evaluate progress, measure what's working, and decide what comes next. Building a trusted, long-term partnership with you is how we make sure your business keeps achieving more — not just at project close, but for every milestone ahead.
