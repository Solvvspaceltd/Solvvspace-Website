What's included
Everything a modern IT department provides — on demand
We take care of the day-to-day so you can focus on your business. No hiring, no training, no employment costs.

Dedicated helpdesk

Named contacts, agreed SLAs, and a ticketing system that gives you full visibility of every issue and its resolution status.

24/7 infrastructure monitoring

Proactive monitoring of your servers, network, endpoints, and cloud services — issues flagged and resolved before your team notices them.

Virtual CTO / IT Director

Strategic IT leadership on a retainer — technology roadmaps, vendor selection, board-level reporting, and investment advice without a full-time hire.

Data backup & disaster recovery

Automated, tested backups with a clear recovery plan. We manage your RTO and RPO so data loss is never a business risk.

IT governance & compliance

Policies, asset registers, software audits, and compliance documentation maintained and up to date — ready for any audit or procurement question.

Onboarding & offboarding

Structured processes for new starters and leavers — accounts, devices, licences, and access provisioned and deprovisioned correctly every time.

Pricing model
Usage-based — pay for what you need
No bloated retainers. No paying for capacity you don't use. Our pricing scales with your organisation.

Helpdesk only

Reactive IT support when your team needs it — per ticket or per user per month. Perfect for businesses with low IT complexity.

Fully managed

Proactive monitoring, helpdesk, and infrastructure management in one package. The most popular option for growing SMEs.

Strategic + managed

Everything in fully managed, plus virtual CTO engagement, quarterly roadmap reviews, and board-level technology advisory.
