Why Cyber Essentials matters
It's not just a certificate — it's a commercial requirement
More organisations — including UK government suppliers — now require Cyber Essentials as a condition of doing business.

Required for government contracts

Any business supplying to UK government must hold Cyber Essentials certification. We'll have you compliant and ready.

Reduces your insurance premium

Many cyber insurance providers offer lower premiums to Cyber Essentials certified organisations — often covering the cost of certification itself.

Demonstrates to clients you take security seriously

Certification signals to prospects and partners that your organisation has baseline security controls in place — a growing procurement requirement.
